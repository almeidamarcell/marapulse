Chameleon Principle

  • Client's color controls: trigger button, voted upvote state, trigger shadow tint.
  • Everything else stays monochrome. Black CTAs, black active chips, black focus, neutral text.
  • The widget feels native to each product while maintaining consistent UX structure.
  • Client configures: accent color, board title, subtitle. That's it.
  • "Powered by Marapulse" is the only Marapulse branding inside the widget. Terracota appears only on hover of that link.
  • Board title is configurable: "Feedback", "Ideas", "Feature Requests" — whatever fits the client's voice.
  • Subtitle also customizable per board.
